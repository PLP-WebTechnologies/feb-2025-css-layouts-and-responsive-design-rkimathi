About Our Milk

We source our milk directly from the best dairy farms in Kenya's highlands, where cows are pasture-fed and treated with care. Our milk is processed within hours of milking to ensure maximum freshness and nutritional value.

With our vending machines located across Nairobi, you can enjoy farm-fresh milk at affordable prices, available 24/7.
